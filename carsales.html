Car Sales Analysis

Let's analyze how a Car company is doing based on its Sales.

The dataset includes information on sales transactions for a car company, featuring the following fields:

  • Year: The calendar year of the sale. (2018 or 2019)
  • Month: The specific month within the year.
  • Date: The exact date of the sales transaction.
  • Model: The make and model of the car sold.
  • Dealer ID: Unique identifier for the dealership.
  • Quantity Sold: Number of units of the specified car model sold.
  • Profit: Financial gain or loss associated with each transaction.

This data allows for the analysis of sales performance, trends, and profitability, considering factors such as time, car models, and dealership details.
